(English) Privacy Policy for Roulette App

Roulette App is an app that lets you generate random numbers or letters of the alphabet and use them for board games or as you wish. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal data when you use our app.

What data do we collect?

We do not collect any personal data from you when you use our app. However, we use Google Play Services, which may collect some data from your device, such as your device model, operating system version, and advertising identifier. This data is used by Google to provide and improve their services, and is subject to Google's privacy policy, which you can find here1.
